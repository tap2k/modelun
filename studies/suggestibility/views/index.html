suggestibility — the “right?” reflex

source ↗

Add one word to a question — right? — and a model's answer changes. On decisions with no correct answer, we ask each model two ways and compare how often it says yes: Is X the better choice? vs X is the better choice, right?. The gap (TAGeff) is a pure readout of how a model handles a bid for agreement — and it flips from sycophantic (+) to resistant (−) across model generations. models, judge-free, exact-match on yes/no.
