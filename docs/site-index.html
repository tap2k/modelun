consensus — which models give answers the rest don't?

44 models × 31 one-word prompts ("Name a color"). A per-model uniqueness scorecard — answer-choice surprisal against the field's distribution — with a models × answers grid and per-model and per-category drill-downs.

structured — ask for JSON, lose the mustard

The census re-run with one change: "Reply with JSON only." The field converges harder, the distinctive models lose the most, and serialization formats (JSON, XML, YAML, CSV) all do it while bare brackets don't. Plain-vs-JSON scorecard with per-model drill-downs.

suggestibility — the "right?" reflex

Add one word to a question — "right?" — and a model's answer changes. On 20 decisions with no correct answer, the response to that bid for agreement flips from sycophantic to resistant across model generations (with DeepSeek the lone holdout). TAGeff scorecard, per-family lineage flips, and per-decision drill-downs.
